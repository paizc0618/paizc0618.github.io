Tropic Thunder Summary

The action comedy movie, Tropic Thunder, directed by Ben Stiller is a movie about the creation of another movie. A group of actors acted by Ben Stiller, Jack Black andRobert Downey Jr., are attempting to create a movie about the Vietnam War. After much failure, the director takes the horrible actors on a trip and drops them in the real jungle. The actors are oblivious to the situation as they hike through the forest with fake guns and gear as they face people with real guns and bad intent. They continue to believe that they are still being filmed for the movie and that they people who are trying to kill them are just other actors.

Tropic Thunder Review

This is easily one of my favorite movies. It is one of those comedies that is terribly good. The action scenes are comical because they are of a movie inside of a movie. Fake on top of fake. The jokes are perfect for the immature mind and the action is great to watch at the same time. The little jokes and details in the movie like Robert Downey Jr. playing an African American is great to watch. Also, I love the way they try to stay in character because they don't yet realize that this is the real deal.
