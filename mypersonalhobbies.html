Computers

I spend a lot of time working with computers. I currently create different things in programs such as photoshop, after effects, illustrator, Cinema4D, etc. In Photoshop and Illustrator, I create logos and company rebrands, banners, etc. In After Effects, I work on video creation and I am going to lean more about animation. And in Cinema4D, I create 3D models and 3D scenes, but I do this less often because of how CPU instensive it is. I also play games on my computer in my freetime with some of my friends. I also sometimes work on building computers for my friends, family or myself. I really enjoy this hobby because I love to create things. The designs I make, the 3D landscapes, the video editing is something I love to do because I enjoy creating things that others can look at as a sort of art form. Also, client work earns me a few "paychecks" here and there. I strive to be a creator with the group Obey Studios. It's a small group of like-minded motion-graphic creators that I wish to be a part of some day. This is a video of one of my older projects that I worked on for Obey: My Project

Golf

I also play golf in my freetime. I have been golfing since I was about 5 years old, but I didn't start playing regularly until my middle school years. I made the team at CCA both Freshmen and Sophomore year and I am hoping to return to the team this spring as a Junior. Golf is something that has become very relaxing and enjoyable whether I go out by myself with some music, or I go with my family members or friends.
